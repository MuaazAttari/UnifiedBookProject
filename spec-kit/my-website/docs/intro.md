# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the Unified Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Learning Book. This comprehensive textbook covers the fundamentals and advanced concepts of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics.

## What You'll Learn

- Fundamentals of Physical AI
- ROS 2 concepts and implementation
- Gazebo and Unity simulation
- NVIDIA Isaac platform
- Vision-Language-Action (VLA) systems
- Conversational Robotics
- And much more!

This book is designed to take you from beginner to expert in the field of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics.
